There's no hope

It may be my fault, but I can't.

Every day I woke up wishing I were dead. Yes, I'm a coward who's never willing to take any responsibility, and I'm a dope who can do nothing but endless complain. I desperately want to be loved, just like an infant.

I'm negative and useless.

These are not "bad thoughts" , rather, these are truths I have to face. I was kind and weak, that's why I had always been bullied. I was young, that's why I shouldn't feel melancholy. I want to drink and smoke every day, just like my dad does, for it helps alleviate my endless pain by providing me with a moment of euphoria. But I can't. Because I don't make money, and I don't have a fiancée.

Nevertheless, present never bothered me as future did.

What am I going to do after graduating from college? To be a nobody and earn a wage so little that barely do I make ends meet? Or to be a runaway living like a refugee and forgotten by the world?

Not to mention my mental illness never stops troubling me.

I covet love, which I don't and (firmly believed) I won't deserve and get. There used to be someone who's kind enough to care for me, yet how could I possibly deserve such an awesome and caring girl like her?

My life's been set. Alone, unloved, coward, mentally suffering...... Come on I am an epitome of loser and bad guy!

Just fuck it.

:-(

Whether 'tis nobler? To struggle in vain and lie to myself? Or to escape from this harsh world and into the unknown?

I know I should not do this, but, I wish there'll be someone, like Anny, who would care for me, encourage me and help me out of this dark abyss.

Si tu es là, prouve-le-moi, rassure-moi, emmène-moi !
